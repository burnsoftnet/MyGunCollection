Data Loader Application  

    This application will update or add to the My Gun Collection application on pre-loaded data from our database. This will include Manufacturers, Models, Grips, Maintenance Plans,Calibers and More to help you fill quickly fill out information on your firearm collection.  Some people like have a listing of all the Manufacturers, Models, and Calibers available for easy selection as they their collection grows.  But there are some that will not find this application useful. 

NOTE: This will only download information from our server, none of your information is submitted over the web.

To access the Data Loader/Data Pre-Loader application just click on Tools, Database and Data Pre-Loader.  Which will bring up the following window.

Click on Next to continue

By Default All is selected, but if you are not interested in getting all the data, then you can select individual sections that you are interested in. 
NOTE:  The Firearm Models will take a while to down load due to the mass amount of firearm model listings that we have in the database.

When you are finished, click on Next to Download the data and apply it you database.  The Next two screen will occur without you having to do anything, so sit back and watch, grab a cup of coffee and/or go reload some ammo.

Once the Download is complete and the updates have been applied, the following screen will appear.

Now just click on Exit to end the application.  Now if you open up the sections that you selected, you should notice extra data in there.   This is something that you will not have to update a lot.  New Manufacturers and Models may get updated every 6 months.
